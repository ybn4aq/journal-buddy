Welcome to Journal Buddy

Journal Buddy is a daily journaling website that promotes better wellness. Having a good day? Journal about it! Describe your day in detail, or just go over the highlights. Have a picture? Throw that in, too! If you're having a rough day, we'll remind you of your past good days and what you enjoy doing.

What are you waiting for? Start your journey towards a better mental health today!

Created by Helen Yao, Larissa Cybyk, Neal Langhorne, and Thomas Arnold for the HooHacks 2024 competition.
